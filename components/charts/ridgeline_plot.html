The voice of dissent
Weekly distribution of protests by type (“peaceful protest” and “violent demonstration”) over the years.

The charts reveal the frequency and intensity of civil mobilizations: on one hand, the desire for freedom and rights; on the other, the anger that explodes into clashes. Wider curves indicate weeks of intense political or social activity: a sign of the ongoing tension between resistance and repression.
The streets remain one of the few spaces where the population can still make their voices heard.
Violent protests, while present, represent a a smaller part of the whole: the majority of the population still chooses to express their discontent in a non-violent way.
