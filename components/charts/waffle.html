In depth analysis by country

Analyzing the composition of violent events reveals a disturbing trend: the number of attacks directly targeting civilians, such as sexual violence, attacks, abductions, or forced disappearances, is constantly growing, overcoming other traditional forms of violence such as armed clashes, suicide bombs, drone strikes or missile attacks. These graphs show how violence is not the same everywhere. But everywhere it is civilians who pay the price, who have become the real battlefield of the Middle Eastern conflict.
